Crypto Currency Research

Listed below are the 3 Crypto Currencies that I researched. Feel free to check them out!

Bitcoin

Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that operates on a technology called blockchain. It was created in 2009 by an anonymous person or group known as Satoshi Nakamoto. Unlike traditional currencies, Bitcoin is not controlled by any central authority like a government or bank. Instead, it relies on a network of computers to process and verify transactions

Ether

Ether

Ether, often abbreviated as ETH, is the native cryptocurrency of the Ethereum blockchain. It serves as the primary fuel for executing smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps) on the platform. Much like gasoline powers a car, Ether powers the computations and transactions within the Ethereum ecosystem.

Tether

Tether

Tether, often represented by the ticker symbol USDT, is a prominent cryptocurrency classified as a stablecoin. Unlike other cryptocurrencies that exhibit significant price volatility, Tether is designed to maintain a stable value, typically pegged to the US dollar. This stability makes it a popular choice for traders and investors seeking to mitigate risk and facilitate seamless transactions within the cryptocurrency ecosystem.
